About This Webpage!

Hi everyone and welcome to my Full House fan page! This website is all about celebrating one of the best family sitcoms of all time. Full House gave us laughs, life lessons, and unforgettable characters like Danny, Jesse, Joey, D.J., Stephanie, and Michelle. Every episode reminds us how important family, love, and sticking together really are.

Favorite Character!

My favorite character on the show is Michelle Tanner because she is funny, adorable, and always steals the show. Even though she is the youngest, Michelle has some of the most memorable moments and catchphrases, like You got it, dude! She always says what she is thinking, which makes her scenes extra funny.
